## 6. Industry Trends

### App Adoption Explosion
- **46% YoY** rise in downloads for astrology, live rituals, and e-puja apps (Pheonix Research)
- Mobile spiritual users grew from 60M (2020) to 90M (2023) (DharmikVibes)

### AI Integration
- AstroSage AI: 90% margins, 20% MoM growth for 18 months
- Multiple AI astrology startups funded in 2024-2025 (Vaya, MyNaksh, Melooha)
- AI enables diagnosis at near-zero marginal cost

### Online Puja Becoming Mainstream
- **105+ online puja startups** tracked on Tracxn
- Temple administrations implementing online darshan/booking
- Sri Mandir: 30M downloads, 2.7M offerings facilitated
- DevDham: 5,000+ temples across 16 states
- Utsav: 400+ temples, 500K+ users

### Video Commerce & Live Streaming
- Live aarti streaming, virtual puja via video call becoming standard
- Online pujas 50-66% cheaper than in-person (accessibility + affordability)

### Startup Ecosystem Boom
- **900-951 religious/spiritual tech startups** in India (Outlook Business)
- Sector funding: $88.5K (2015) → $50.7M (2024) = **573x growth**
- AstroTalk IPO (late 2026) will be category-defining event

### NRI Diaspora as Premium Segment
- 30M+ Indians living abroad
- Sri Mandir: 700K registered users outside India, growing 15% QoQ
- NRI ARPU 10x domestic ($81 vs $7-9)
- Willing to spend significantly on remote temple services and spiritual products
